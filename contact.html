Submission Guidelines

Before you send

  • Keep written submissions under 200 words unless agreed otherwise.
  • Images should be clear and original — no screenshots of other people's work.
  • Include your name (or initials if you want to stay anonymous) and class.
  • Content should be relevant to nature, environment, or sustainability.
  • The editor reserves the right to lightly edit for clarity and length.
  • Submissions may be featured in print or online versions of the newsletter.

For Teachers & Staff

Stakeholder outreach

The Green Gene is a CBSE SEWA project under the Environment Conscious Citizens strand. If you are a teacher, parent, or school authority interested in supporting this initiative, please reach out via email.

We are also open to suggestions for future issue themes, collaboration with eco-clubs, and support for implementing greywater and zero-waste initiatives documented on this site.

People for the Planet

Conservationists, scientists, and authors who are fighting for the Earth

JG
Conservationist

Jane Goodall

Primatologist & UN Messenger of Peace · UK

Spent 60+ years studying chimpanzees in Gombe, Tanzania. Changed how science understands animal intelligence and emotion. Now travels 300 days a year advocating for forests and wildlife.

"You cannot get through a single day without having an impact on the world around you. What you do makes a difference."

GT
Activist

Greta Thunberg

Climate Activist · Sweden

Started the global Fridays for Future school strike movement at age 15. Addressed world leaders at COP conferences. Has since shifted to broader climate justice advocacy.

"Our house is on fire. I want you to act as if your house is on fire, because it is."

DA
Scientist · Author

David Attenborough

Naturalist & Broadcaster · UK

Created landmark nature documentaries including Planet Earth, Blue Planet, and A Life on Our Planet. At 98, still actively advocating for biodiversity protection worldwide.

"No one will protect what they don't care about, and no one will care about what they have never experienced."

SS
Conservationist · India

Sunita Narain

Environmentalist & Director, CSE · India

Heads the Centre for Science and Environment in Delhi. Has fought for air quality standards, vehicle emission norms, and water rights in India for over three decades.

"Pollution is not just an environmental problem. It is a social justice problem."

VM
Activist · India

Vandana Shiva

Environmental Philosopher · India

Founded Navdanya, a movement to protect seed diversity and organic farming in India. Has fought against GMO monocultures and corporate control of agriculture for 40 years.

"The earth is not a resource. It is an heirloom."

WM
Scientist

Wangari Maathai

Environmental Activist & Nobel Laureate · Kenya

Founded the Green Belt Movement, which planted over 51 million trees across Africa. First African woman to win the Nobel Peace Prize (2004) for her work linking democracy and environment.

"In the course of history, there comes a time when humanity is called to shift to a new level of consciousness."

RK
Author · India

Ruskin Bond

Author & Naturalist · India

India's beloved author has written about the Himalayan forests, rivers, and wildlife for 70 years. His writing builds deep love for nature in generations of Indian readers.

"The trees are my oldest friends. They have seen more of life than I shall ever see."

VT
Conservationist · India

Valmik Thapar

Tiger Conservationist · India

Has spent 50 years studying and protecting tigers in Ranthambore. His campaigns were instrumental in expanding tiger reserves and raising awareness about poaching.

"The tiger is the barometer of India's ecological health."

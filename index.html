About this newsletter

The Green Gene is a quarterly environmental newsletter created as part of the CBSE SEWA (Social Empowerment through Work Education and Action) programme. It covers sustainability news, eco tips, quizzes, and reader submissions — all focused on making green living accessible to students.

Published from Patna, Bihar. Editor: Ayushman.
